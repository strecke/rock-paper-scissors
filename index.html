About This Project

<3 Thank you for playing!

It was a lot of work, plenty of Googling, reading docs, browsing forums, and yes, sometimes looking at AI search results, but it was an absolute blast to build!

I adapted the W98 styling by Jordan, but chose not to use the pre-build stylesheet to maximize my own learning.

I also extensively used the online v86 emulator by Fabian as a reference.

Feel free to contribute on GitHub! :)
